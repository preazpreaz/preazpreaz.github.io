Alejandro's Top Five Favorite Albums

10. Some Rap Songs by Earl Sweatshirt

After a long awaited 3 years since his last project, Earl Sweatshirt's 2018 magnum opus Some Rap Songs is a true display of lyricism and story telling. In this album, Earl dives into vunerable themes such as his struggle with mental health following several losses in his life, including his father and uncle who had helped raised him. Paired with avant-garde jazz, this album is a perfect blend that makes the border between dreams and Hip-Hop dizzy.
